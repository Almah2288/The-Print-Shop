About The Print Shop

This fab lab is fully equipped with 7 printers and all the materials needed to complete projects. Access the lab Monday through Saturday. Meet other people and connect with the tutors, laptops availabe in lab.

The Print Shop is a fab lab located in the heart of silicon valley, it is the first high-tech location dedicated to education, free resources an materials. Everyone can learn 3D printing software step by step. Tutors are available to teach the software required to make projects.

As new 3D printers continue to become available worldwide. The Print Shop will keep innovating and improving, always striving to be a global industry leader and carry the torch for education.
